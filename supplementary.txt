Bubbz cmd line, $g$ contains all input genomes:

	bubbz -k 21 -b 300 -m 200 -a 150 -t 24 $g

For lines below, $r and $q are $i-th and $j-th genomes of the dataset.

Minimap2 cmd line for mapping different genomes:

	minimap2 -D -cx asm5 -t24 $r $q > $dir/minimap_$i\_$j.tmp

Minimap2 cmd line for mapping same genome to itself:
	
	minimap2 -DP -k19 -w19 -m200 -t24 $r $r > $dir/minimap_$i\_$i.tmp

Mashmap2 cmd line for mapping different genomes:

	mashmap -r $r -q $q -t 24 -o $dir/mashmap_$i\_$j.tmp -f one-to-one

Mashmap2 cmd line for mapping same genome to itself:

	mashmap -r $r -q $r -t 24 -o $dir/mashmap_$i\_$i.tmp -f none
